Our Process

Ideation and documentation

Before we start a project, we make sure that our experts understand your requirement accurately and then a clearly defined document with measurable milestones and plan of action is created.

Development

During development, our team keeps you updated on the work progress and milestones. We ensure that we have regular meetings with our client to keep them in the loop about the progress of their projects.

Testing and deployment

When a project is complete, our experts test it and then a prototype is given to the client for testing. Once the testing is complete and the project is approved, the project gets deployed.

Maintenance

We offer 24/7 support after the project is live. We understand that your projects are important to you and so we guarantee quick resolution.
